📄 Product Requirements Document (PRD)
🤖 AI Chatbot for eCommerce Websites
🧾 1. Product Overview

Product Name: AI Chatbot for Business Websites
Type: Web-based AI Assistant
Platform: eCommerce Websites

📌 Description

An intelligent chatbot integrated into eCommerce platforms that assists users with:

Product discovery
Customer support
Order assistance
Personalized recommendations

The chatbot simulates a human-like shopping assistant to improve engagement and conversions.

🎯 2. Goals & Objectives
🎯 Business Goals
Increase conversion rates 📈
Reduce customer support workload 🤝
Improve user engagement 💬
🎯 Product Goals
Provide real-time responses
Deliver accurate product recommendations
Ensure smooth user interaction
👥 3. Target Users
🛍️ Primary Users
Online shoppers
First-time visitors
🧑‍💼 Secondary Users
Business owners
Customer support teams
💡 4. Problem Statement

Users on eCommerce websites often:

Struggle to find relevant products
Have unanswered queries
Drop off due to lack of support

👉 Solution:
An AI chatbot that provides instant, intelligent assistance like a real store assistant.

🧠 5. Product Features
🔹 Core Features
1. 💬 Conversational Chat
Real-time messaging
Handles FAQs (returns, shipping, pricing)
2. 🛒 Product Recommendation
Suggest products based on user queries
Example: “Best phone under ₹20,000”
3. 🔍 Smart Product Search
Natural language understanding
Filters products dynamically
4. 📦 Order Support
Track orders
Return/cancel guidance
🔹 Advanced Features
AI-powered responses (LLM integration)
Voice interaction 🎤
Multi-language support 🌍
Personalized suggestions
Chat history
⚙️ 6. Functional Requirements

FR1	System must accept user messages

🚫 7. Non-Functional Requirements
